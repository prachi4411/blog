Summary

DescriptionHyderabad's beautiful Charminar, on a lovely sunny morning.jpg
English: The Charminar is one of those monuments that you don't realize how beautiful it is until you see it up-close. One of the reasons is that the road around it is usually packed with pedestrians and vehicular traffic. But early one morning in 2010, I made a trip to try and catch a sight of this magnificent monument unencumbered with traffic around it, and it paid off. This is a rare picture of Charminar without a crowd around it, and without any scaffolding or chipping paint. I took this picture from within the premises of Unani Hospital nearby. It is my favorite picture of Charminar, and not only because I took it. :-)
